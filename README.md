# Milkshake Mania

> **Welcome to Milkshake Mania!** You've just opened your very own milkshake stand, and it's time to blend your way to milkshake greatness! This guide will teach you everything you need to know to become the ultimate milkshake mogul.

---

## Quick Start Guide

Ready to start blending? Here's what to do:

1. **Click the Blend Button** - This is your main action! Each click makes a milkshake.
2. **Watch Your Money Grow** - Each milkshake you make earns you cash ($).
3. **Unlock Upgrades** - Spend your earnings on upgrades to make more money per blend.
4. **Hire Employees** - Let them make milkshakes while you're busy!
5. **Expand Your Empire** - Keep upgrading and hiring until you're the milkshake tycoon!

---

## How to Play

### Milkshake Blender

The **Milkshake Blender** is your best friend. Every click:

- Creates a delicious milkshake
- Earns you money

The blend time starts at **7.5 seconds per blend**, but you can upgrade this to blend faster!

### Making Money

There are two ways to make money in Milkshake Mania:

| Method              | Description                                                                                  |
| ------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Manual Blending** | Click the Milkshake Blender to make milkshakes yourself. You earn 100% of the profits!       |
| **Auto-blend Mode** | Enable the auto-blend mode in the top left corner, and it saves the hassle of spamming.      |
| **Employees**       | Hire workers to make milkshakes for you. They work automatically but earn less individually. |

### The Upgrade Shop

Press the **Upgrades** tab to see all the ways to improve your operation:

- **Blend Speed** - Blend faster (reduce that 10-second timer!)
- **Employee Training** - Your employees become more efficient
- And a lot more, so many that they can't be listed here

### The Employee Shop

Press the **Employees** tab to hire help. Although you get passive income, the tradeoff is that every month in game, where each day is a second, you must pay their salaries.

> 💡 **Tip:** Employees are great for earning money while you're away or busy clicking!

---

## Special Outcomes

When you blend a milkshake, there's a chance it turns out extra special! These give you bonus money:

| Outcome     | Chance | Bonus         |
| ----------- | ------ | ------------- |
| **Crusty**  | 10%    | +$10 bonus    |
| **Baked**   | 5%     | +$25 bonus    |
| **Swirled** | 1%     | +$100 bonus   |
| **Golden**  | 0.1%   | +$1,000 bonus |

> 🎉 These special outcomes can stack! You might get a "Golden Swirled" milkshake worth even more! Technically, if you are extremely lucky (0.000005%, or **1 in 20 million**), then you can even get a **"Crusty Baked Swirled Golden Shake!"**

### How to Get Better Outcomes

The **Golden Milkshake** is the rarest and most valuable, excluding stacking. You have a 0.1% chance for each blend to be Golden

## Game Screens

### Milkshake Panel (Main Screen)

- Your milkshake blender, which you click to _make a shake_.
- Current money and earnings per second
- Progress bar for current blend

### Upgrades Tab

- All purchasable upgrades
- Shows current level, cost, and effect
- Grayed out if you can't afford it

### Employees Tab

- All employee types you can hire
- Shows how many you own and their total output
- Costs increase as you hire more

### Shop Extensions Tab

- All purchasable shop extensions
- Shop extensions to increase employee capacity

### Stats Panel (in the Milkshake Panel)

- Total money earned
- Total milkshakes made
- And more!

### Settings Tab

- **Export Save** - Copy your save code to share or backup
- **Import Save** - Load a saved game from a code
- **Hard Reset** - Start completely over (Warning: This erases all progress!)
- Don't worry if you don't export your save though, as the game automatically saves to _localStorage_,
  which only gets wiped when you clear your cache

## Economy & Progression

### Early Game (Start - $5,000)

Focus on:

- Clicking the Blend Button frequently (or use the auto-blend feature)
- Buying the first few upgrades
- Hiring a few Student Interns
- Expanding the employee capacity

### Mid Game ($5,000 - $100,000)

Focus on:

- Upgrading Blender Power for bigger payouts
- Hiring Milkshake Artists and Shake Scientists
- Working toward the more expensive upgrades

### Late Game ($100,000+)

Focus on:

- Higher level staff, e.g. a Chef
- Maximing out your upgrades
- Improving and leveling up some of the upgrades, which raises the chance of getting
  **Crusty**, **Baked**, **Swirled** and **Golden** shakes.
- Watching your income and money grow!

## Tips & Tricks

### 1. Be Patient

The game is called "incremental" for a reason. Progress might feel slow at first, but it accelerates as you upgrade!

### 2. Don't Neglect Upgrades

Upgrades often give better returns than hiring more employees early on. Check your Upgrades tab often!

### 3. Watch for Sales

Some upgrades might feel expensive at first. Come back later and check again!

### 4. Employees Are Great

Even though employees earn less than manual clicking, they work 24/7! They're perfect for building wealth over time.

### 5. Special Outcomes Add Up

Even though Crusty and Baked milkshakes are common, they add up over time. Every bonus helps!

### 6. Save Often

Your game auto-saves, but you can manually save anytime. Export your save code to a text file for backup!

### 7. Frequent Updates

The game is regularly updated with new features, improvements, upgrades, and exciting content, so make sure to
check back often and don't miss out!

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q: How do I earn money faster?**
A: Buy upgrades first! Shop Popularity increases your per-blend earnings, while Blend Speed lets you click more often.

**Q: Should I hire employees or buy upgrades?**
A: Early game, upgrades give better value. Mid to late game, employees become more important for passive income.

**Q: What happens if I close the game?**
A: Your progress is automatically saved! Just open the game again and you'll continue right where you left off.

**Q: How do I get Golden Milkshakes?**
A: Buy the "Golden Touch" upgrade in the Upgrades tab. Then you have a small chance each blend!

**Q: Can I play on my phone?**
A: Yes! The game is playable on mobile devices. Simply tap the **Blend Button** just like you would click it on a computer.

**Important:** You may need to lower your browser zoom/scale depending on your device. The game is still being optimized for smaller screens and may not display properly on lower resolutions or high zoom levels.

Also, just to be clear, there’s obviously no way to play the Windows desktop version of the game on a phone. Mobile support only applies to the browser version of the game.

**Q: What's the rarest outcome?**
A: **Golden** at 0.1% chance! But with enough blends, you'll start seeing them.
